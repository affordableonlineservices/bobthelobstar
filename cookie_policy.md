## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and store certain information about your activity. Other technologies that work similarly to cookies — including pixels, local storage, and software development kits (SDKs) — are treated the same way under this Policy.

Cookies can be:

- **First-party:** set directly by the website you are visiting (bobthelobstar.com).
- **Third-party:** set by other organizations whose services are embedded in the website (such as analytics or advertising providers).
- **Session cookies:** deleted automatically when you close your browser.
- **Persistent cookies:** remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

## 4. Your Choices

You can control cookies in several ways:

### 4.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers let you view, delete, and block cookies. Common browser controls can be found here:

- **Chrome:** [https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- **Firefox:** [https://support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- **Safari (macOS):** [https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- **Safari (iOS):** [https://support.apple.com/HT201265](https://support.apple.com/HT201265)
- **Edge:** [https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

Blocking strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from signing in or using the Service.

### 4.2 Cookie Banner

Where required by applicable law (for example, for users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and California), we present a cookie banner that allows you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the cookie preferences link in our website footer.

### 4.3 Advertising Opt-Outs

You can limit interest-based advertising from participating companies through the following industry tools:

- **Digital Advertising Alliance:** [https://optout.aboutads.info](https://optout.aboutads.info)
- **Network Advertising Initiative:** [https://optout.networkadvertising.org](https://optout.networkadvertising.org)
- **European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance:** [https://www.youronlinechoices.eu](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu)

You can also opt out of personalized advertising directly on the platforms themselves:

- **TikTok ad settings:** available in the TikTok app under Settings → Privacy → Ads.
- **Meta ad settings:** [https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences](https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences)

### 4.4 Mobile Device Opt-Outs

Mobile operating systems provide their own controls for limiting ad tracking:

- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track."
- **Android:** Settings → Privacy → Ads → enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

### 4.5 Do Not Track

Our website does not respond to "Do Not Track" ("DNT") browser signals because no common industry standard exists for interpreting them. You can still use the controls described above.
