## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information:** email address, chosen username, and password. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt) and are never viewable by us in plaintext.
- **Conversation content:** the messages you send to BoB, the responses BoB generates, and any business context, preferences, files, or personal information you voluntarily include in conversations. This is what powers BoB's persistent memory feature.
- **Payment information:** when you subscribe, payment is processed by our payment provider (Stripe, Inc.). We do not receive or store your full credit or debit card number. We receive and store limited information such as your subscription status, the last four digits of your payment card, billing zip code, and billing cycle.
- **Communications with us:** any email, support request, or other communication you send us.

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- **Usage data:** login timestamps, features used, session duration, pages visited, and other product-interaction data.
- **Device and log data:** IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers, referring URL, and similar technical data.
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** see Section 5 (Cookies and Tracking Technologies) below.

### 2.3 Information From Third Parties

- **Analytics and advertising partners:** we use conversion-tracking pixels and SDKs from third parties (including the TikTok Events API and the Meta / Facebook Pixel) that may receive information about your interactions with our website and our ads, including events such as page views, signups, and subscriptions. These partners may use cookies and similar identifiers to associate your activity on our site with their broader tracking network.
- **Payment processor:** Stripe returns data about the status of your payment and subscription to us.

### 2.4 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not knowingly collect:

- Sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, government identification numbers, biometric data, or precise geolocation.
- Information from individuals under the age of 18.

If you believe you have provided sensitive information by accident, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active, and for a limited period afterwards, as follows:

- **Account and conversation data:** retained while your account is active. If you delete specific conversations or clear your memory from the dashboard, that content is removed from our active systems. If you delete your account, we delete your account and conversation data within 30 days, subject to the exceptions below.
- **Payment and billing records:** retained for up to seven (7) years to comply with tax, accounting, and anti-fraud obligations.
- **Logs and security records:** retained for up to 24 months for security and abuse-prevention purposes.
- **Backup copies:** may persist for a reasonable additional period before being overwritten.
- **Legal holds:** we may retain information longer if required by law or relevant to an actual or anticipated legal matter.

After the applicable retention period, we delete, anonymize, or aggregate the information so that it can no longer be associated with you.
